Our World: Deteriorating

Climate change.
I am sure that you have heard of this phrase numerous times. Its poularity is growing day by day and little to none action is being taken place to prevent the harm of our planet. I would like for you to imagine this, people stuck in their homes because of the scorching heat, pollution laying astray on streets, humans and animals alike not being able to get the nourishment they require because of the shortages in clean water. I know that these images are not pretty sights.
This might be the fate and state of future generations' daily lives. As a matter of fact, these are only a few of the difficulties approaching Earth. In the bottom bullet list, I have noted down more problems which are interlinked with climate change.

  • Droughts
  • Storms
  • Heat Waves
  • Rising Sea Levels
  • Warming Oceans

Aside from the list above, pollution is a whole another case! Not only does Earth get attacked by Climate change, pollution also exists, and we need to halt this damage which is done to Earth. It surprised me a lot when I realised that the same people that depend on Earth to live are also killing Earth. This does not benefit anyone!
The three main types of pollution are listed underneath:

  1. Land Pollution- Trash littering the land which leads to a loss of fertile land for agriculture.
  2. Water Pollution- Trash in water which sometimes results into sea animals eating it and falling sick or dying.
  3. Air Pollution- Harmful gases mixing with the clean gases in air which can cause respiratory damages.

Finally, a website named Climate.gov stated that tempratures had been rising about 0.08 celsius per decade since 1880. However, since 1981, the temprature had been rising more than double that amount per decade: 0.18 celsius!
In addition, this website also stated that 2021 had been the 6th warmest year on record!
If this trend keeps continuing, it will be barely any time before we are stuck at our homes all year with barely any snow around the world. This is no joke.
Even with all these downsides, hope is being able to see that there is light despite all of the darkness and there is definitely some hope remaining.
